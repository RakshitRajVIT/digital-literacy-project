# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone  
- Always verify the identity of unknown callers or messages  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security patches  

## UPI & Financial Safety (Important)
- Never approve a "Collect Request" unless you are sure you are paying someone  
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter your PIN to receive money  
- Double-check UPI IDs and names before sending money  
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: **1930**  

Staying alert and informed can help prevent most cybercrimes.
